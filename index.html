Welcome to LlamaCTF, a new cybersecurity conference located in Arlington TX, USA.
Our goal is to use Capture-The-Flag events to inspire anyone new to cybersecurity
 or are close to graduating college.

We are using the CTF platform MetaCTF for the event and cyber professionals will 
be available to provide guidance and assist with the event.

We recommend attendees have a few things off this list:
- Familiarity with Linux.
- Familiarity with web-pentesting, CTF events, or related concepts.
- Basic knowledge of forensics concepts.
- Curiousity and a desire to learn.

To get some practice we suggest:
- PicoCTF
- HackTheBox
- TryHackMe
